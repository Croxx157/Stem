Paleoclimatology

Paleoclimatology is the science that studies Earth's past climate, before instrumental records existed. Paleoclimatologists analyze proxies such as ice cores, tree rings, sediments, and stalagmites to reconstruct past climate conditions and understand natural climate change patterns.
